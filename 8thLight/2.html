# Why are you pursuing a career in software development?

I'm pursuing a career in software development because I love writing software!

I had some interest in programming as a kid (I wanted to be a video game developer at the time), but was intimidated by my initial introduction to it (some dense Java book) and didn't end up getting into it right away. Many years later, I found myself working in a position that required a lot of manual data entry/manipulation. I knew that there had to be a better way to get that work done, so I took it upon myself to learn how to automate routine tasks in Excel with functions & VBA. Once I got started, I was hooked! I loved being able to augment my abilities and get work done hundreds/thousands of times faster. It seemed like a form of time travel, where I could conjure up some script and jump ahead to a point in the future where I had done hours & hours of tedious work, without actually having to do it myself.

Once I got past that initial barrier, I found myself wanting to learn more and did so by working on an endless queue of personal projects. I started off with Python; writing automation scripts & extending SublimeText to improve my development workflow, and also prototyping a few app ideas with Kivy. Then I got into mobile app development for a while, mostly working with Java & Kotlin in Android Studio. While looking for a more efficient way to develop cross-platform apps, I tried out a few options like Xamarin & Flutter before finally settling on web development as my primary focus after finding out about PWAs and the very expansive, developer-friendly ecosystem that web development offers. I've also experimented a bit with Unity & TouchDesigner, and am very interested in interactive audio-visual experience design.

Most of my programming experience has been gained from working on personal projects, and most recently from working as a (solo) contracted developer at a local eCommerce company. I'd love an opportunity to work with other developers to build bigger projects than I could manage on my own, and would very much enjoy the learning opportunities & growth that come along with that. I especially resonate with & appreciate 8th Light's principles; working for a company that values & promotes growth, learning, & excellence is pretty much my ideal career scenario!
